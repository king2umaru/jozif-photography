Hi, I am Joseph.

Joseph Godfrey, Visual Artist, Tech Enthusiast & Creative Director

Joseph godfrey is a Canon Ambassador and has won several awards in Nigeria and Africa including Future Awards Prize for Photography 2018 and The ASFA Fashion Photographer of the year (Africa) 2018, Fashions Finest Award for Fashion Photographer of the year 2019 and many others.

His portfolio includes editorials for some of the country’s most distinguished designers. Works for other brands include Coca Cola, Zaron Cosmetics, GTBank, Samsung, Williston Northampton School, Unilever, Darling Nigeria to mention a few and has been featured on CNN Africa Avantgarde.

Notable commissions and collaborations include Martell, Jameson, Absolut, Canon, Tecno Mobile, Mitsubishi Motors and UNICEF.

He is the founder and creative director of Zen Studios, a fully equipped photo studio and creative workspace located in Ilupeju, Lagos. He is also the director of Zen Photographics, a photography/creative solutions provider and servicing company whose primary objective is to provide photographers and creatives with homegrown innovative solutions to all their creative needs.

Other commissions include official portraits for the President of the Federal Republic of Nigeria, campaigns for Polo Avenue, Netflix, GTBank, Amazon Prime, Coca Cola, Radisson Hotel Lagos and many others.
